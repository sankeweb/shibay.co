THE BIRTH OF SHIBAY

SHIBAY (sheebay) was founded by T. Shiraz Sheriffdeen in 2005. The name SHIBAY was formed as a nick name given to the Managing Owner Mr. Shiraz as Shibe, short form of Shirazbebe in Dhivehi. being a master Designer and a Businessman he was dealing with various trades in Male' for Government, Private and Individual sectors and became well known among Maldivians. Moving to Sri Lanka with family for better prospect, he continued the trade by supplying the needs of Maldivian customers from Sri Lanka. The frequent business encouraged him to open his own Company and registered the name Shibe as SHIBAY in December 2012. The important fact behind this name is also derived from eBay (multinational), iBay in Maldives and SHIBAY in Sri Lanka. Although its ba​sed in Sri Lanka, the nature of the business is to provide services and Online Marketing to the Maldivians in Sri Lanka and Maldives itself.
